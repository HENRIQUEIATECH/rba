Commitment and Social Impact

The Human Challenge

Many Brazilians face loneliness and isolation, which can lead to depression. In remote areas, access to simple channels of health and cultural information is limited, creating an emotional divide.

Technology Nearby

RBA is a bridge. Developed in native Java and weighing only 5.1 MB, it democratizes mental health culture and services, working on any device.

Results in Lives

We integrate podcasts on physical and mental health, as well as legal guidance. Hearing users say "I didn't know our Brazil was so rich" validates our mission to combat loneliness

Our Story

"Imagination is more important than knowledge..." – Albert Einstein

We create software for all types of needs, using advanced Java programming for structured applications and small to large projects, with integrated design and art.

The company's structure is based on two main pillars: Paulo Salomão, focused on entrepreneurship consulting for its users; and IArtium, entirely focused on technology.


RBA was born from the desire to rescue and preserve the sonic soul of our country. More than a radio station, we are a living museum. The RBA software was designed under the concept of Native Performance. Developed 100% in native JAVA to guarantee lightness and extreme speed.

RBA apps are free and will always keep your audio repertoire growing without the need to download another new app.
